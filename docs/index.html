Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

Built to be fast, lightweight, and distraction-free. Detection runs entirely on your device in real time.

🔴

Real-Time Detection

EfficientDet-Lite0 AI model detects traffic lights in every camera frame — fast and accurate on any modern Android phone.

🔔

Smart Audio Alert

A sharp high-frequency beep fires the moment a traffic light enters the frame. Loud enough to hear over road noise.

🚗

Speed Aware

Alerts automatically pause when you're stationary. GPS speed gates the alert so you won't be bothered at a red light.

📡

Fully Offline

The AI model is bundled inside the app. No internet connection needed, ever. Works in tunnels, rural areas, anywhere.

🔍

Built-in Zoom Controls

Floating + / − buttons let you adjust zoom while driving. Starts at 1.5× by default for a longer detection range.

📱

Screen Always On

The display never dims or locks while the app is open. Close the app and normal screen timeout resumes automatically.

How It Works

Three steps, zero setup

No accounts, no configuration, no cloud. Install, open, drive.

1

Mount your phone

Place your phone in a windshield or dashboard mount with the back camera facing the road ahead.

2

Open the app

Grant camera and location permissions. The app starts scanning instantly — no login, no settings required.

3

Drive and listen

A sharp audio alert fires every time a traffic light is detected while you're moving. Stay focused on the road.
