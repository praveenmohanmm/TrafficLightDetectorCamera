Privacy Policy

App: Traffic Light Detector  |  Last updated: April 2026

Traffic Light Detector is a camera-based app that detects traffic lights in real time and plays an audio alert when one is detected. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All processing happens entirely on your device. No images, video, location data, or any other information is ever sent to us or any third party.

2. Permissions Used

  • Camera — Used to capture the live camera feed for on-device traffic light detection. Camera frames are processed in memory only and are never saved, recorded, or transmitted.
  • Location (Fine & Coarse) — Used solely to read your current speed from the GPS sensor. The app uses speed to pause alerts when you are stationary. Your location coordinates are never stored or transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the MediaPipe on-device machine learning library (by Google) to run the traffic light detection model. All inference runs locally on your device — no data is sent to Google or any other service as part of this process.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository .
